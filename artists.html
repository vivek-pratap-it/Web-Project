Pradip Sarkar

Pradip was born in Dhanbad state of Jharkhand. He has been getting recognized in the Indian contemporary art since 1995. He is a commerce graduate from Ranchi University and he holds a diploma in fine art from British Institute, Mumbai.
Pradip has received certificates and merit cash awards, The Hindustan Times, New Delhi – 1995 and has been nominated under India 5000 best MSME award for individual contributors in the sectors of visual art – 2017 and 2018.
Pradip has held 5 solo exhibitions and participated in more than 35 group shows at significant venues. This includes exhibitions such as with M. F. Hussain, Prince of Wales Museum – Mumbai, Lalit Kala Academy – Bhubaneswar, Academy of Fine Arts – Kolkata, Jehangir Art Gallery – Mumbai, Indian Academy of Fine Arts – Amritsar, World Trade Centre – Mumbai, etc. He has worked at various national and international events and has participated in many camps, workshops and live demonstrations across the country. He has also created a large theme sculpture in open air for the academic complex, IIT, ISM Dhanbad – Jharkhand – 2006.
His works are at collections in Germany, USA and India and they have also found place in the collections such as Lalit Kala Akademi - Bhubaneswar, The Hindustan Times - New Delhi, IIT Dhanbad, INSCOL Pvt. Ltd. - Chandigarh, LODHA COSTIERA - Mumbai, ABHIJEET BHATTACHARJEE, ANU MALIK and SHARDA, (Bollywood personalities), ANU SUBRAMANIAM, USA and many more.
Pradip presently lives and works in Mumbai.
